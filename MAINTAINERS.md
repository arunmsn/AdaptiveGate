## Becoming a Maintainer

Criteria:

1. Sustained contributions over at least 3 months
2. Deep familiarity with the layered architecture and dependency rule
3. Demonstrated good judgment in PR reviews
4. Nominated by an existing maintainer and approved by majority vote

## Responsibilities

- Review and merge PRs within 5 business days
- Triage issues
- Cut releases following the process in `scripts/release.sh`
- Uphold the non-negotiables defined in the PRD
